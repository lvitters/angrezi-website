Radio Angrezi is a collective radio station founded by students of the HfK Bremen. We are understanding the radio as a platform of a constant learning-process as well as an open platform for everybody in- and outside of HfK. Everyone is invited to reach out and contribute their own program — live as well as pre-recorded. Anyone interested in seeing the process of radio making, is invited visiting our cozy studio, as well as listening space in Speicher XI, Segment 7, next to HfK Bremen.


Contact Radio Angrezi:

You can write us ideas about any kind of contribution or just to say hi at: radioangrezi@hfk-bremen.de
