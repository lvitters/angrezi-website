Welcome to Radio Angrezi!

A collective of people from Bremen, Leipzig, Karlsruhe and Venezia has arrived on Lido de Venezia. Are still arriving. Will yet arrive. A summer school is taking place. The Biennale is making space. Beyond Repair.
